Library NFO.Morphs: Morphisms

In this module we prove that:
  • iin, Ain, Bin are eeq-morphisms.
  • Ain and Bin are respectively Aeq- and Beq-morphisms.
  • The axiom of extensionality for Aeq and Beq. Extensionality for iin and eeq will be proved in NFO.Ext.


Proofs for Aeq and Ain


Lemma Aeq_Ain: ∀ {X Y} {f: X → _} {g: Y → _},
  Aeq f g → ∀ x, Ain x f ↔ Ain x g.
Lemma Ain_Aeq: ∀ {X Y} {f: X → _} {g: Y → _},
  (∀ x, Ain x f ↔ Ain x g) → Aeq f g.

Theorem Aext {X Y} {f: X → _} {g: Y → _} :
  Aeq f g ↔ ∀ x, Ain x f ↔ Ain x g.

Proofs for Beq and Bin


Lemma Qeq_Qin: ∀ {X Y} {p p'} {h: X → _} {h': Y → _},
  Qeq (map h p) (map h' p')
  → ∀ x, Qin x h p ↔ Qin x h' p'.

Lemma Qin_Qeq: ∀ {X Y} {p p'} {h: X → _} {h': Y → _},
  (∀ x, Qin x h p ↔ Qin x h' p')
    → Qeq (map h p) (map h' p').

Theorem Qext {X Y} {p p'} {h: X → _} {h': Y → _} :
  Qeq (map h p) (map h' p')
  ↔ ∀ x, Qin x h p ↔ Qin x h' p'.

Lemma eeq_Qin: ∀ {x y A p} {h: A → _},
  x == y → Qin x h p ↔ Qin y h p.
